Sushi restaurant comes to Smiths Falls



“There is no sushi restaurant in this area,” said the restaurant’s chef and owner Ryan Chung, who is working alongside his co-owner, wife, Julie Jang, and son, John Chung, to open the highly anticipated restaurant.

“Sushi is probably the most popular foreign food,” said Ryan, who also cooks a wide range of Asian cuisines. “I could do Korean, Chinese, Japanese sushi, but the most popular is sushi. Canadians love sushi.”

The globe-trotting family has recently settled in Smiths Falls and are looking forward to being a staple in the local restaurant scene.

This is Ryan’s second time owning a restaurant. The first restaurant he opened was in Los Angeles. The family, who is from Korea originally, has lived in Beijing, China, then they moved to Los Angeles, then Chicago, then to the Bracebridge in the Muskoka region, then Cobourg, and finally Smiths Falls.
